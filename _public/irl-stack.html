In Real Life (IRL) Stack

Devices, apps, and services I use day-to-day.

Hardware

Computing

  • HP Probook 430 G1 - Daily driver
  • Schok Classic - Daily driver. It has all the basics and a little extra since it's Android Open Source Project (AOSP) based.
  • Surface Go - Secondary device
  • Surface Duo - Secondary device
  • Surface Laptop
  • Pinebook Pro
  • Raspberry Pi 4

Bags

  • Patagonia Atom Sling 8L - Every Day Carry (EDC) bag
  • Osprey Farpoint Travel Pack 55 - I use the large backpack for larger trips and the daypack is the main backpack I use for day hikes.

Photography & Video

  • Canon PowerShot SX740 - Mainly for photos
  • Akaso Brave 7 - Action cam. I usually use it to record outdoor trips. It's also compact enough for every day carry to supplement my phone's camera.
  • Logitech C920S Pro HD Webcam - Webcam for video calls & streaming

Audio

  • FiiO M6 - MP3 player. Mainly used for podcasts
  • JLAB JBuds Air ANC - Daily driver
  • Surface Earbuds

Routers

  • GL.iNET Beryl
  • GL.iNET Mango - Travel router
  • GL.iNET Mudi - Hotspot

Miscellaneous

  • Garmin Fenix 6 Pro - Fitness tracker & GPS device
  • Garmin Drive 52 - Car GPS
  • Pocketbook Touch Lux 5 - E-Reader

Software

Operating Systems

  • Manjaro - Daily driver
  • Windows
  • Ubuntu
  • Debian
  • Android

Audio

  • AntennaPod - Podcasts on Android
  • RadioDroid - Internet radio
  • MusicBee - Manage podcasts on Windows & transfer them to my MP3 player (FiiO M6).
  • Spotify - Exclusively for music. It would probably be easier to use it for both music and podcasts but for some odd reason, I like managing music & podcasts separately.

Communication

  • Matrix - Chat
  • Jami - Chat
  • Jitsi - Video calling
  • Discord - Chat
  • Slack - Chat
  • Skype - Audio & Video calling

Feed Reader & News

  • Newsblur - If it has an RSS feed, it's here. Blogs, news sites, YouTube channels, podcasts, etc. I use NewsBlur as the one place to manage my RSS feeds. I also have these RSS feeds in apps like AntennaPod & MusicBee for podcasts but NewsBlur is the source of truth in case I decide to switch podcast apps in the future. Also, NewsBlur provides you with an e-mail address so you can subscribe to e-mail newsletters.

Notetaking

  • Joplin - I really like authoring content in Markdown. Joplin lets me do that for notes while also giving me flexibility when it comes to syncing.
