Welcome to my curated collection of RSS Starter Packs.

Inspired by BlueSky's Starter Pack feature, I've created a set of OPML bundles to help you discover and follow content across the open web. Whether it's a newsletter, blog, podcast, YouTube channel, Fediverse instance, or BlueSky profile—if it has an RSS feed and aligns with the topic, it's included.

By leveraging open standards like RSS and OPML, my goal is to:

  • Make it easy and convenient for others to discover, explore, and subscribe to content using their own feed readers
  • Promote the open web and open standards. When building on open standards, it doesn't matter what platform or software you use. Both publishers and subscribers have the freedom to choose.
