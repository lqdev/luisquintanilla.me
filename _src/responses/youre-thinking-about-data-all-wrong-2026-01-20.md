> C. Thi Nguyen talking about how we let others define meaning for us:  
> <br>
> > Someone says, “Here’s a watch. It measures your health.” And if you accept that, you’re letting someone else — maybe like Apple [for example] — define what health means for you. And they typically define it by what is easy for one of their devices to measure cheaply.
