> As an Emacs user, few things are as delightful as catching my favorite text editor out in the wild. It doesn’t happen often though – Emacs is niche, and pop culture rarely gives it a nod. This post tracks down every one I know of (as of June 2026), and I’ll keep adding to it as I stumble across more. The main list covers on-screen sightings (movies, TV series) along with comics and manga. Textual references in books are collected separately under “Honorable mentions”.
