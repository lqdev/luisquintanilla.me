A living document that tracks what's currently active, what's being learned, and what's on the horizon — across all my work, not just any one project. Updated as projects and interests evolve.

## Active Projects

- **[Mycelium](https://github.com/lqdev/mycelium-mvp)** (TypeScript) — federated agent orchestration on decentralized social protocols (AT Protocol, ActivityPub, Matrix). My flagship exploration at the intersection of AI agents and the open social web.
- **[copilot-emacs](https://github.com/lqdev/copilot-emacs) + [copilot-sdk-elisp](https://github.com/lqdev/copilot-sdk-elisp)** (Emacs Lisp) — an AI-native Emacs powered by GitHub Copilot, plus an Emacs Lisp SDK (ACP transport, tools, sessions, BYOK). Making the editor itself agent-native.
- **[podcast-tui](https://github.com/lqdev/podcast-tui)** (Rust) — a terminal UI for podcast management.
- **[podcast-scrobbler](https://github.com/lqdev/podcast-scrobbler)** (C# / .NET 10) — a self-hosted, ListenBrainz-compatible scrobble server. I own the whole data path.
- **[lqdev.me](https://github.com/lqdev/luisquintanilla.me)** (F#) — my online home; an IndieWeb static site generator. Ongoing: `/now-playing`, a `/tools` directory, `/colophon`, and privacy-aware quantified-self primitives.
- **Personal web tools** — small utilities I actually use: a [read-it-later extension](https://github.com/lqdev/read-it-later-browser-extension), a [GitHub-post PWA](https://github.com/lqdev/github-post-pwa), an [RSS extension](https://github.com/lqdev/rss-browser-extension), and a [playlist creator](https://github.com/lqdev/playlist-creator).
- **AI Memex** — this knowledge-capture system: an AI companion that helps me write down patterns, research, and decisions as I work.

## Learning Goals

- **AI and agents** — how autonomous agents reason, coordinate, and act, especially when federated across open protocols rather than locked to a platform.
- **The open social web** — the protocol layer: AT Protocol, ActivityPub, Matrix, Webmention, and how they interoperate.
- **AI-native developer tooling** — agent runtimes, editor integration, and SDKs (e.g. driving Copilot from Emacs).

## Current Interests

- The **open social web** — IndieWeb, blogging, RSS, and open protocols. Owning my identity and my data online.
- **AI agents** at the intersection with that open web (the Mycelium thesis).
- **AI-native tooling** — treating AI as a first-class collaborator and building the tooling to make that real.
- **Self-hosting and decentralization** — Matrix, Owncast, my own scrobbler, decentralized knowledge networks.
- **Quantified self, digital gardens, and commonplace books** — capturing and resurfacing what I read, listen to, and learn, without surrendering it to a platform.
- **Privacy and security** — first-class design constraints, not afterthoughts.

## On the Horizon

- Bringing `/now-playing` to life with real-time scrobble data, and proving out the "private vault → public garden" privacy model before extending quantified-self to reading, watching, and places.
- Promoting my radio playlists into a first-class **stations** content type.
- Continued progression of the Mycelium MVP toward real federated agent workflows.
