## Overview

A wiki is a publication on subjects related to a particular topic. It often serves as a knowledge base.

## Examples

- [Wikipedia](https://www.wikipedia.org/)
- [IndieWeb Wiki](https://indieweb.org/wiki)
- [Arch Wiki](https://wiki.archlinux.org/)
